About

I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at POSTECH Institute of Artificial Intelligence. During my Ph.D. studies and postdoc, my research has focused on Energy-Efficient Digital System Design under the supervision of Prof. Youngjoo Lee. More specifically, my research interests include:

  • Hardware-software co-design and co-optimization
  • Energy-efficient DNN inference in edge devices (Low bit-quantizaiton + Sparsity)
  • Ultra-Reliable Low-Latency wireless Communication (URLLC) system
  • ASIC implementation and verification
