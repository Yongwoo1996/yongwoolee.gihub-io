About

I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at UNIST EE under the supervision of Prof. Jimin Kwon. During my Ph.D. studies, my research has focused on Flexible/Printed Bioelectronics under the supervision of Prof. Sungjune Jung. More specifically, my research interests include:

  • Intelligent reflective surface (IRS) for 5G/6G
  • Printed ion-gated FET RF varactors
  • Organic neuromorphic devices
  • Wearable/implantable biosensors
  • Monolithic 3D oxide semiconductor FETs for Memory
