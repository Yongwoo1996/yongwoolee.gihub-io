About

I am a postdoctoral researcher at POSTECH EPIC Lab. As a hardware design engineer and researcher, I am interested in energy-efficient hardware systems for DNN inferences, wireless communications, and signal processing. My research mainly focuses on HW architectures, SW-HW co-optimizations, and ASIC/FPGA implementations at the digital circuit level. I received the B.S., M.S., and Ph.D. degrees from POSTECH EE, Republic of Korea, in 2018, 2020, and 2024, respectively. I was extremely fortunate to have been advised by Prof. Youngjoo Lee while pursuing my PhD.

Research Interests

  • Designing HW-friendly algorithms for large-scale DNN inference
  • Efficient HW architectures for AI and signal processing applications
  • SW/HW co-optimization
  • Next-generation Wireless communication systems
